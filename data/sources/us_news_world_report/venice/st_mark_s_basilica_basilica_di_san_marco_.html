How we rank things to do

This elaborate church sits on the popular piazza by the same name. The church was built in honor of St. Mark the Evangelist, whose remains were stolen in Alexandria, Egypt, by a couple crafty Venetians, intent on bringing him to rest in Venice. (What made them crafty? They smuggled the remains to Italy in barrels of pork fat.) 

Crafted from many different styles of architecture, today the basilica's opulence shines from nearly every corner – from the four full-size bronze horses that guard the entrance to thousands of square feet of mosaics to the Pala d'Oro — an elaborate altar piece commissioned in Byzantium by the Venetians. If you look closely at the center gable on the exterior of the basilica, you can even see a statue of St. Mark along with Venice's emblem: a lion with wings. 

Many consider the basilica to be a must-see attraction. Others said that though the lines to enter tend to get long, they move pretty quickly. To avoid the heavy crowds, heed the advice of past travelers and plan to arrive right when the church opens. Or, sign up for one of the best Venice tours; many offer skip-the-line access to the church.

Keep in mind that backpacks aren't allowed inside the basilica and photography/videography is prohibited.

Hours

The basilica is open from 9:30 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. (last access given at 4:45 p.m.) Monday through Saturday; on Sunday, it's open from 2 to 4:30 or 5 p.m. The basilica's museum is open daily from 9:30 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. (last access given at 4:45 p.m.).

Dress Code

Clothing should also be appropriate for a place of worship, meaning you should have knees and shoulders covered.

Tickets

Admission to the basilica is 3 euros (about $3.30), but to enjoy the museum, you'll pay 7 euros (about $7.70); entrance to the Pala d'Oro costs 5 euros (around $5.50). Climbing the bell tower (which is open from 9:30 a.m. to 9:15 p.m. daily) will set you back 10 euros (around $11). Children younger than 6 are free for all attractions. For more information, visit the church's website.
