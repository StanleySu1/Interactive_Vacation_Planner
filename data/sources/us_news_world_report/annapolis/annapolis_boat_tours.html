How we rank things to do

With 533 miles of shoreline, one of the best ways to explore Annapolis is by boat. You'll get to see Annapolis in its natural beauty and see popular landmarks such as the U.S. Naval Academy, Thomas Point Shoal Lighthouse and the Chesapeake Bay Bridge. 

There are numerous tours and operators in Annapolis that provide a variety of experiences to travelers. A majority of boat tours are seasonal, typically running from March to the end of October. The following are a few travelers' favorites: 

Classic Sail Charters: Classic Sail Charters provides a private charter sailing adventure of the Chesapeake Bay. A group of 2-6 people can enjoy a 2 1/2 to three-hour cruise (depending on the time of day) around Annapolis. Past visitors of Classic Sail Charters say that boating with the company is a unique experience thanks to the scenic views and excellent professionalism of the staff. Pricing ranges from $495 to $750. Proof of COVID-19 vaccinations is required. The minimum age of passengers is 7 years old. 

Watermark Cruises and Tours: According to reviewers, Watermark Cruises and Tours provides a fantastic experience with beautiful views. Watermark provides numerous Annapolis cruises, anywhere from sightseeing cruises to a daylong cruise from the Chesapeake Bay to St. Michaels, another historical town located across the Chesapeake Bay on Maryland's Eastern Shore. Boat tours can last from 40 minutes to 7 1/2 hours. Prices for adults range from $22 to $99 and for children ages 11 and under, prices range from $7 to $35. 

Schooner Woodwind Annapolis Sailing Cruises: Family-owned Schooner Woodwind Annapolis Sailing Cruises takes guests on a sailing tour of the historic Annapolis waterfront and the Chesapeake Bay. The sailing company's two wooden 74-foot schooners, Woodwind and Woodwind II, provide daily cruises as well as sunset and specialty cruises featuring everything from historical Annapolis to holiday themes. The two-hour daily cruise sets sail four times a day. Travelers will have to check the company's website for the schedule for sunset sails and specialty cruises. The daily cruise price ranges from $71.95 to $75.25 depending on the day and time a day you set sail. For sunset and specialty cruises, prices may vary. 

Family Fishing Adventures: Kid-friendly, fish-approved Family Fishing Adventures is an interactive fishing outing. The 1 1/2-hour tour takes kids on a catch and release fishing excursion where they learn to fish, crab and preserve the Chesapeake Bay. During the season, the company operates daily, five times a day. Reservations are required. There is a 12-person maximum per tour.  Prices range from $20 to $30. 

To learn more about boat tours in Annapolis, visit the tour operators' individual sites or the tourism board's website.
