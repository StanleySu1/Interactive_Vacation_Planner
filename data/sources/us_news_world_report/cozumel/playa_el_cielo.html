U.S. News Insider Tip: Playa El Cielo is best known for its proximity to the El Cielo Reef, which is a sanctuary for sea animals that is only reachable by boat. Pack plenty of reef-safe sunscreen if you're planning a full day here. – Holly D. Johnson

Playa El Cielo is a stunning white sand beach that is well known for its crystal-clear waters that are home to starfish, stingrays and other marine animals. Visitors can book a range of water tours here that can help them explore under the sea by boat, and all with the help of a professional guide. The beach also has a sandbar water lovers can swim out to.

Note that the beach isn't accessible by car (there are no roads leading to it), so most visitors get here on a boat to begin with. Those staying in Cozumel can book any number of tours to visit the beach with companies like Cozumel Best Tours and Leon Tours Mexico, yet it's also a popular cruise ship excursion for day trippers. Either way, visitors agree that the water is clear and warm and that you can see all kinds of wildlife once you’re out in the sea. Also note that, if you opt to spend the day on the beach instead of snorkeling, you can rent chairs and order food and drinks.

The beach itself is around 16 miles south of San Miguel de Cozumel. Tour prices and inclusions vary. You can also visit the beach for free if you are able to travel there by boat on your own.
