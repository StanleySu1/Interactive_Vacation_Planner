How we rank things to do

For dining and shopping in the heart of Malibu, the Malibu Country Mart is a great outdoor mall with lovely landscaping and seating areas. It sits within walking distance to attractions like Malibu Pier, Adamson House and Surfrider Beach, which makes it an easy place to grab a coffee before heading to those spots. You might even spy a famous actor or musician at the precinct's celebrity-frequented Starbucks.

Marmalade Café is a favorite for fueling up before and after hikes or enjoying a leisurely brunch. Lucky's Steakhouse consistently has diners raving, while Café Habana (opened by supermodel Cindy Crawford's husband Rande Gerber) is a fun spot for drinks. Travelers have mixed reviews on its Cuban/Mexican fare, but the cocktails are a hit. For retail therapy, there's chic eyewear, aromatherapy, accessory and clothing stores like John Varvatos, Andie Swim and Brandy Melville, however the designer stores and boutiques can be pricey.

Those traveling with kids will be happy to hear there's an outdoor playground.

Malibu Country Mart is located about half a mile west of Malibu Pier and has a large free parking lot as well as street parking on Cross Creek Road. If using public transport, take the No. 134 bus from Santa Monica and disembark at the Cross Creek/Malibu Theatre stop.

Hours

The stores are generally open daily from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m.; restaurant hours may vary.

For more information, visit the official Malibu Country Mart website.
