Lake Wallenpaupack spans 13 miles in length, making it the third-largest (human-made) lake in Pennsylvania. Take your pick of ways to get out on the water: swimming, boating, kayaking, paddleboarding, Jet Skiing and wakeboarding are just some of the activities you can enjoy from outfitters like Wallenpaupack Scenic Boat Tour & Boat Rentals. On land, you can hike the Wallenpaupack Lake Trail, explore the Lacawac Sanctuary (a nature and environmental education center) or relax on the shores of Palmyra Township Public Beach.

Extend your time in the area by renting a cabin on the lake, or book one of the waterfront cottages at Silver Birches Resort.Â
